Welcome to Ray-Ban

What is the reason and aiming to achieve behind this Project?

There are millions of people who are suffering from visual impairment in the world and their numbers are increasing day by day. We can easily understand how hard their lives are when we put ourselves in their shoes. As a leading brand in sun and prescription eyewear for generations, we had to show our empathy for those people and find a way to help them which is the reason we come up with this idea. Our main goal in this project is to show people a life without vision even for a few seconds and raise the awareness about people visually impaired.
